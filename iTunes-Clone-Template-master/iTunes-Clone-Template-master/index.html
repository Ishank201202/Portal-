INTERNATIONAL ENGINEERING PROGRAMS

University of Toledo (UToledo)
UToledo is one of the most comprehensive universities in the USA. They offer more than 270 degree programs teaching real-world skills that prepare you for the competitive, global job market, that prepare you to make a difference in your community. University of Toledo, in collaboration with Thapar Institute of Engineering & Technology, invites outstanding students to participate in a combined-degree program in the engineering. Upon successful completion of the first 2 years of the Bachelor of Engineering at Thapar Institute of Engineering & Technology in the following majors: • Bio-Medical Engineering • Computer Science and Engineering
University of Queensland
There is an agreement with University of Queensland under the credit transfer program offered in 2+2* (2+2.5 for Civil Engineering) mode. By awarding credit, a student reduces the amount of learning required to achieve a qualification. Credit may be awarded through an arrangement or process of credit transfer, articulation, recognition of prior learning or advanced standing.
University of New South Wales
The Pathway is expected to involve students studying a total of two (2) years/ Four (4) semesters fulltime at TI (TI Requirements) and a minimum of six (6) terms fulltime at UNSW, subject to them satisfying progression requirements at all stages. Students who have successfully completed the TI Requirements will commence their studies at UNSW in the third year of the UNSW Bachelor of Engineering (Honours) program and will be awarded the degree of Bachelor of Engineering (Honours) by UNSW, on completion of all remaining UNSW requirements.
